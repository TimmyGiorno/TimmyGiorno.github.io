A succinct yet elegant personal profile

1. English Version

Hello!

My surname name is Zhao, you can also call me Timmy or Gio. Gio is an Italian surname transmitted from Chinese since many of non-Chinese native speakers can’t pronounce this Chinese surname correctly. This profile focuses more on all aspects of my life, career, and hobbies. If you are more interested in my academics and business, feel free to read my résumé.

I am from Hulunbuir, and I attended Hailar Second High School for my high school education. Currently, I am studying at Wuhan University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence. I am also affiliated with the Institute of Precision Measurement Sciences at the Chinese Academy of Sciences, specifically in the Neuroimaging Data Analysis department. During the spring of 2023, I am doing a visiting scholar program at the University of California, Berkeley, and I had originally planned to do research at the University of Toronto during the summer.

My background began in chemistry competitions, but I have since transitioned my focus. I am actively researching or have researched topics such as Cognitive Neuroscience, Neuroimage Analysis, Computer Vision, Comprehensibility, Quantum Physics, and Natural Language Processing. I have a strong foundation in mathematics and science, with a focus on Brain Science and Computer Science. Currently, my main research is centered around image registration in Computer Vision. I have already published two papers, one of which is as a second author in a top-tier journal in the first quadrant of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

I am currently collaborating with major corporations in relevant fields and preparing patent projects. I expect to establish my own AI Ltd. within the next 1 to 2 months.

Recently, I have been involved in the development and creation of indie games as a full-stack developer. I have previously managed a Minecraft server for over seven years. Currently, I am leading an indie game studio, working on an independent game inspired by Faulkner’s The Sound and the Fury.

I am a polyglot, fluent in multiple languages. My native language is Chinese, and I have varying levels of proficiency in English, German, Italian, Finnish, Latin, Russian, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Romanian, Estonian, Hungarian, and more. While my vocabulary may not be extensive, I focus on understanding the structures and nuances of languages for linguistic purposes. I have a keen interest in historical comparative linguistics and support Chomsky’s transformational generative grammar theory. I achieved a GRE score of 329 (159+170) and scored 91 on the TOEFL in my sophomore year.

I identify as both a Marxist and an Existentialist. My favorite philosophers include Protagoras, Karl Marx, Nietzsche, Wittgenstein, and Žižek. I strongly adhere to Epistemological Relativism and Moral Relativism. I am more interested in instrumental rationality than discussions of right or wrong.

Among my favorite authors are Kafka, Dostoevsky, Faulkner, Sholokhov, and Joyce. While I am not particularly drawn to Proust’s literature, I still consider In Search of Lost Time a great work. Similarly, I view Solzhenitsyn as a staunch Pan-Slavist and Russophile, yet recognize the significance of The Gulag Archipelago and Red Wheel.

In my free time, I enjoy artistic pursuits, particularly in classical music. My favorite composers include Rachmaninoff, Brahms, Edvard Grieg, Bartók, Mahler, Einojuhani Rautavaara, and Beethoven. I have a modest understanding of visual arts and 3D modeling, mostly for personal enjoyment. My favorite artists are Goya, Eugène Delacroix, Matisse, and Duchamp. I have achieved a grade ten proficiency in piano, which I would describe as requiring significant effort but attainable. Recently, I have been practicing pieces such as Brahms’ Piano Concerto No. 1 in D minor, Grieg’s Piano Concerto in A minor, Debussy’s Arabesques, Clair de Lune, and Rêverie, and Rachmaninoff’s Lilacs, among others. I have also self-taught myself the flute, recorder, and Irish whistle, although my proficiency in these instruments is not on par with the piano. I am nearing completion on two compositions: Six Piano Lyric Pieces and a symphonic poem.

Lately, I have been deeply immersed in watching films, despite having seen relatively few in the past. I have watched all of Stanley Kubrick’s films and particularly admire A Clockwork Orange and 2001: A Space Odyssey. Among Chinese directors, Jia Zhangke is a favorite of mine.

I consider myself an intermediate to high-level gamer. In League of Legends, I am a Diamond support player with a 70% win rate on Anivia. I primarily play mid and jungle roles, avoiding assassins. In Teamfight Tactics, I am a double Master in Solo and Duo modes. In Apex Legends, I achieved Platinum III in the tenth season, primarily playing as a second position in the team with an average of 550 damage per match. I have been playing StarCraft for twelve years, though I admit my skill level is not particularly high. In the past, I have served as a forum moderator for an unknown gaming forum (in fifth grade) and as the administrator of a Counter-Strike private server (in fourth grade). I do not play mobile games and am not a console gamer. Additionally, I was an active content creator on various platforms from 2005 to 2013, excluding platforms like Kuaishou and Douyin.

My favorite sports are soccer, volleyball, and swimming, and I engage in regular fitness activities. I am a dedicated fan of the Golden State Warriors, Miami Heat, and the Kobe-era Los Angeles Lakers in basketball. In soccer, I support both Manchester City and Liverpool, with a deep admiration for Lionel Messi.

It’s a pleasure to meet you! You can find my contact information on my profile, and you’re welcome to reach out anytime.

2. 中文版本

我姓赵，也可以叫我 Timmy 或 Gio。

我来自呼伦贝尔，高中在海拉尔二中。武汉大学-人工智能系-本科在读，中国科学院精密测量研究院-脑科学研究所-影像学数据分析。2023 春季在 University of California, Berkeley 访学，原计划暑期在 University of Toronto 做 research。

我以化学竞赛出身，已转行，正研究或研究过 Cognitive Neuroscience / Neuroimage / Computer Vision / Comprehensibility / Quantum / NLP，数理基础扎实的 Brain Science & CSer。目前主要研究 CV 的图像配准（Image registration）。目前已有两篇论文在 Google Scholar 上收录，其中一篇二作为中科院一区 TOP 期刊。

目前正与利益相关大企业通力合作，准备专利项目，预计在 1~2 个月之内将会拥有自己的 AI Ltd.。

我最近在搞独立游戏（Indie Game）的开发与创作，全栈开发者。我曾经运营过超过七年的 Minecraft 服务器，目前正在主理一个独立游戏工作室，以 Faulkner 的《喧哗与騷乱》为灵感制作一部独立游戏。

我是多语言者（Polyglot / Multilingual）。我的母语是汉语，有听说读写能力的外语有 English(英) / Deutsch(德) / Italiano(意)（能力自高而低排列），有读写能力的外语有 Suomi(芬) / Lingua Latina(拉丁) / русский(俄) / Français(法) / Español(西) / Português(葡) / Limba Română(罗马尼亚) / Eesti Keel(爱沙尼亚) / 和 Magyar Nyelv(匈)（除了芬兰语和拉丁语有系统学过外大多数都只涉猎一部分，且出于语言学目的而学。词汇量不高，主要了解的是结构和语感）。热爱历史比较语言学，Chomsky 先生和他转换生成语言理论的支持者。GRE 329（159+170），TOEFL 大二首考 91。

我是 Marxist 和 Existentialist。我最爱的哲学家是 Protagoras，Karl Marx，Nietzsche，Wittgenstein 和 Žižek。我坚定 Epistemological Relativism 和 Moral Relativism。我不喜欢谈论是非对错；我只看重 Instrumental Rationality。

我最爱的作家是 Kafka，Dostoevsky，Faulkner，Sholokhov 以及 Joyce。我对于 Proust 的文学不太感冒，但仍觉得《追忆似水年华》是伟大的作品；我同样认为 Solzhenitsyn 是一位彻头彻尾的 Pan Slavist 和 Russophilia，但他的 The Gulag Archipelago 和《红轮》仍是上好的文学作品。

我业余喜欢做一些艺术创作，主要集中在古典乐。最喜欢的音乐家有：Rachmaninoff，Brahms，Edvard Grieg，Bartók，Mahler，Einojuhani Rautavaara, 以及 Beethoven。我会一些美术和3D建模，最多算自娱自乐，最爱的美术家是 Goya，Eugène Delacroix，Matisse 和 Duchamp。钢琴业余十级，水平大概属于弹肖练很费劲但能练下来的程度。最近练习的作品有：Brahms 的 D 小调第一钢琴协奏曲，Grieg 的 A 小调第一钢琴协奏曲，Debussy 的 Arabesques、Clair de Lune、Rêverie，Rachmaninoff 的 Lilacs, etc. 。我自学过长笛，竖笛和爱尔兰哨笛，不过水平远不及钢琴就是了。目前我已经接近创作完成了两部作品：《六首钢琴抒情小品》，以及一首交响诗。

我最近在疯狂看电影，之前看的不是很多。Stanley Kubrick 的电影全看过，最喜欢的是《发条橙》和《太空漫游2001》。国内导演我最喜欢的是贾樟柯。

我算个中高端游戏玩家。LOL 钻石守门员，70% 胜率艾尼维亚，主玩中野，不怎么玩刺客；云顶之弈单排 / 二人赛双大师；APEX 第十赛季铂金三，战队二号位，场均550；十二年星际玩家，但玩的很菜；曾担任不知名游戏论坛版主（小学五年级）和CS私服服主（小学四年级）。不玩手游，非主机玩家。同时，我也是 一位 05～13 年代际的爱拍UP主，不玩快手抖音。

喜欢的运动是足球，排球和游泳，平日健身。篮球最爱勇士、热火和科比时代的湖人；足球是曼城和利物浦双料球迷 + 梅西人迷。

很高兴见到你！我的联系方式在我的主页上，欢迎随时访问。
